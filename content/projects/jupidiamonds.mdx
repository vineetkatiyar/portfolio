### Overview

Jupidia Diamonds is a sophisticated e-commerce platform specializing in custom
jewelry, offering both ready-to-ship pieces and bespoke custom design services.
The platform provides a seamless shopping experience with advanced filtering,
detailed product specifications, and multiple payment options.

### Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React.js, Redux Toolkit, Context API, Tailwind CSS, React Hook
  Form, zod
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database and ORM:** Prisma and Postgresql
- **Authentication:** session, bcryptjs
- **Payment Gateways:** Razorpay, PayPal
- **Image Storage:** AWS S3
- **Admin Panel:** Custom-built with RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)

### Client-Side Features

#### Product Browsing & Detailed Specifications

- **Comprehensive Jewelry Catalog**: Browse rings, necklaces, bracelets,
  earrings, and more
- **Detailed Product Specifications**:
  - Center stones (count, type, carat weight)
  - Accent stones (count, type, arrangement)
  - Side stones (count, placement details)
  - Metal type, purity, and weight
  - Dimensions and measurements

#### Advanced Filtering System

- **Multi-dimensional Filters**:
  - Jewelry Type (Ring, Necklace, Bracelet, etc.)
  - Collection (Engagement, Wedding, Fashion, etc.)
  - Occasion (Wedding, Anniversary, Birthday, etc.)
  - Weight Range (in grams)
  - Price Range
  - Style (Vintage, Modern, Classic, etc.)
  - Metal Color (Yellow Gold, White Gold, Rose Gold, Platinum)
  - Stone Color (D-E-F, G-H, I-J, etc.)
  - Stone Type (Diamond, Sapphire, Ruby, Emerald)

#### Shopping Cart & Customization

- **Size Selection**: Ring size options with international conversion
- **Screw Options**: Customizable screw types for certain jewelry items
- **Gift Cards & Coupons**: Apply discount codes and gift cards at checkout
- **Save for Later**: Option to save items without purchasing immediately

#### Custom Order Creation

- **Custom Design Portal**: Users can submit their own jewelry designs
- **Specification Form**: Detailed form for custom requirements
- **Image Upload**: Ability to upload reference images or inspiration
- **Direct Communication**: Admin contact via WhatsApp for custom orders
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor custom order progress through dashboard

#### Payment Processing

- **Dual Payment Gateway Integration**:
  - Razorpay (popular in India and surrounding regions)
  - PayPal (global payment processing)
- **Multiple Currency Support**: USD, INR, EUR, GBP, and more

### Admin Panel Features

#### Product Management

- **Product Creation**: Add new jewelry items with detailed specifications
- **Variant System**: Manage multiple variants of the same product
  - Different sizes, metals, or stone options
  - Inventory tracking for each variant
  - Separate pricing for variants

#### Automated Pricing System

- **Making Charge Calculator**: Automated calculation based on:
  - Metal type and current market rates
  - Labor complexity and craftsmanship required
  - Stone setting complexity
  - Design intricacy
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Real-time price updates based on material costs

#### Marketing Tools

- **Coupon Management**: Create and manage discount codes
  - Percentage-based discounts
  - Fixed amount discounts
  - Limited-time offers
  - Usage limits per customer
- **Gift Card System**: Issue and track gift cards
  - Purchase and redemption tracking
  - Balance management
  - Expiration date control

#### User Management & RBAC

- **Customer Management**: View and manage customer accounts
- **Role-Based Access Control**:
  - Super Admin: Full system access
  - Content Manager: Product and content management
  - Order Manager: Process orders and updates
  - Support Staff: Customer communication and issue resolution
- **Activity Logging**: Track all admin actions for security

#### Order Processing System

- **Order Dashboard**: View all orders with filter options
- **Status Management**: Update order status (Pending, Processing, Shipped,
  Delivered)
- **Custom Order Workflow**: Special workflow for bespoke jewelry requests
  - Consultation scheduling
  - Design approval process
  - Production tracking
  - Customer communication logs

#### Analytics & Reporting

- **Sales Reports**: Revenue, popular products, seasonal trends
- **Customer Analytics**: Purchase history, preferences, demographics
- **Inventory Reports**: Stock levels, reorder alerts, slow-moving items
- **Custom Order Metrics**: Conversion rates, average value, production time

### Performance Optimization

- **Image Optimization**: Responsive images with multiple resolutions
- **Lazy Loading**: Images and content load as needed

## Project Challenges & Solutions

### Complex Product Variants

**Challenge**: Managing numerous combinations of metals, stones, sizes, and
styles while maintaining accurate inventory and pricing.

**Solution**: Developed a flexible variant system that treats each combination
as a separate inventory item while maintaining connection to the parent product
for display purposes.

### Custom Order Workflow

**Challenge**: Creating a streamlined process for custom jewelry requests that
balances customer creativity with production feasibility.

**Solution**: Implemented a structured submission form that guides users through
providing necessary information, followed by a direct communication channel with
master jewelers.

### International Pricing

**Challenge**: Displaying accurate prices across different currencies and
regions with varying tax structures.

**Solution**: Integrated currency conversion API with geolocation-based pricing
and tax calculation based on customer shipping address.
